Relational Selectors & Combinators

What is a Relational Selector?

Relational selectors target elements based on their relationship to other elements within the markup.

Types of Relational Selectors

Descendant Combinator

The descendant selector targets any element that is a descendant(child, grandchild, great grandchild, and so on) of an element

Example E F Targets any element F that is a descendant of E ol li Targets any 'li' element that is a descendant of 'ol'
